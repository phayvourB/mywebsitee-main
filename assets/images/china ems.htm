So how does this work?

  • Send us your inventory

    Get your manufacturers to mail your inventory to our warehouse.

  • We store your inventory until you receive orders

    Once we receive your goods, we’ll store them safely for you.

  • We prepare your packages when you receive orders

    We’ll pick and pack the products for your orders. Good news, our standard boxes and packaging are FREE.

  • We ship your orders directly to your customers in your name

    Once a day, China Post (they run the ePacket service) will come by to pick up your orders and send them on their way!

  • We update you when the orders have been shipped

    Once the orders have been shipped, we’ll send you a quick update as well as the tracking numbers.

  • You pay us at the end of each week

    Your weekly cost will vary based on a few factors. There are no commitments and no minimums. Get a price quote now.

Essential Information about ePackets

Countries supported by ePacket

As of January 2018, there are a total of 39 countries that have shipping agreements with China Post for the ePacket service, including the US, the UK, Canada, Russia, Australia, New Zealand and a number of countries in Europe and Asia.

View the full list of countries that you can ship to using ePacket.

ePacket shipping restrictions

Unfortunately, there are a few limitations on packages being shipped. Take a look at the weight and size limits of ePacket packages.

Are your packages too big or heavy for ePacket? No problem. Send us a few details and we’ll figure out the best shipping options for you.

ePacket shipping time estimates

(These estimates are valid as of January 2018)
Brazil: 20 to 30 business days
Mexico: 20 business days
Russia, Ukraine and Saudi Arabia: 7-15 business days
All other supported countries: 7-10 business days
